  • Arrive 30 minutes before the event starts
  • Bring identification for ticket verification (virtual tickets only)
  • Small bags, fanny packs, clutches, clear bags no bigger than 12" x 12" x 6"
  • Medical bags allowed but all bags will be searched
  • Possible pat down
  • We allow water containers that are 32 oz or less and empty at entry
